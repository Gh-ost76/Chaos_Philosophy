The Fixed Point

Dharana: Single-Pointed Focus

Ancient yogis gave one instruction that changed everything: "Fix your mind on one object like an arrow on its target." Not ten things, not five things—one thing.

The legendary samurai who never lost a duel in 60 fights would stare at the tip of his sword for hours. Just the tip. Nothing else existed. This practice made his mind "like the tip of the sword, able to cut through anything."
